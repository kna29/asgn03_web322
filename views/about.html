All About Me

I'm Khushi, a 21-year-old currently studying Computer Programming at Seneca. I am born and broughtup in India. My keen interest lies in mathematics and also like to make websites which I am learning in this field, and my goal is to use the programming skills I'm acquiring to make meaningful contributions to this field.


During my leisure time, I find joy in a range of activities. Reading books is one of my favorite pastimes, offering me a diverse array of knowledge and inspiration. Additionally, I express my creativity through baking delightful desserts for my roommates, adding a touch of warmth and togetherness to our shared living space. Music is another passion of mine, especially the soulful melodies of RnB, which bring me comfort and inspiration. Moreover, I also like to do paintings which makes me more happy.


My journey is a blend of structured learning and creative expression. Combining the intricacies of computer programming with my personal interests, I'm navigating a path that is both exciting and full of possibilities. As I progress through the world of code and creativity, I'm eager to see how these experiences will shape my identity and aspirations in the realm of computer programming.
